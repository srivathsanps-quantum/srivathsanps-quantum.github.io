Research

Quantum algorithms for chemistry

Developing and implementing variational quantum algorithms for molecular simulations, including ADAPT-VQE and quantum subspace expansion; benchmarking sampling/statistical errors; and testing workflows on IBM Quantum hardware.

Scalable excited-state modeling

Applying subspace methods and related techniques toward accurately modeling challenging excited states where classical approaches struggle.

Quantum machine learning (QML)

Building hybrid quantum–classical models (QNNs, quantum kernels / QSVM-style ideas) using PennyLane + TensorFlow, with careful benchmarking and documentation.
